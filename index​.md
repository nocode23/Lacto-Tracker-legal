# Privacy Policy for Lacto Tracker
Last updated: March 6, 2026

Lacto Tracker (“the App”) is developed to help users track breastfeeding sessions.

## 1. Data We Collect
We do not collect personal data on our servers.

The App stores breastfeeding records locally on your device (for example: session start/end time, duration, and side used).

## 2. How Data Is Used
Your data is used only to provide core app features, including:
- tracking feeding sessions,
- displaying statistics,
- showing widgets and Live Activities.

## 3. Data Sharing
We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties.

## 4. Analytics, Ads, and Tracking
The App does not use third-party advertising SDKs and does not track users across apps or websites.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion
Because data is stored locally on your device, you control it.
You can delete your records directly in the app (if supported) or by uninstalling the app.

## 6. Children’s Privacy
The App is intended for general use by parents/caregivers and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL with the updated date.
