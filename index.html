Privacy Policy for Lacto Tracker

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Lacto Tracker ("the App") helps you quickly log breastfeeding sessions and keep a clear daily overview.

Key Features

  • One-tap start and stop
  • Track left, right, or both sides
  • Session duration and daily totals
  • Statistics for today, last 7 days, last 30 days, and all time
  • Widget and Live Activity support for faster access

Designed for parents who want fast, simple tracking without unnecessary complexity.

Important: Lacto Tracker is not a medical software and does not provide medical advice.

1

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data on our servers. The App stores breastfeeding records locally on your device.

2

How Data Is Used

Your data is used only to provide core app features (tracking, statistics, widgets, Live Activities).

3

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties.

4

Analytics, Ads, and Tracking

The App does not use third-party advertising SDKs and does not track users across apps or websites.

5

Data Retention and Deletion

Data is stored locally on your device. You can delete it in the app (if supported) or by uninstalling the app.

6

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for parents/caregivers and does not knowingly collect children's personal data.

7

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version is always available at this URL.
